Doner Kebab

Description

The world-famous Turkish kebab means ‘rotating’ kebab in Turkish. It is made from lamb, chicken, or beef, slowly roasted on a vertical spit, and then thinly sliced off. Döner is served on a plate with salad and rice or potatoes; in bread like a sandwich; or in a wrap known as dürüm.

Ingredients

  • Ground meal
  • Onion
  • tomato

Steps

  1. Make the marinade
  2. Score the lamb shoulder's fat in a crosshatch pattern, being careful not to pierce through the flesh.
  3. The next day, let the lamb rest at room temperature, still tightly covered in foil, an hour before cooking.
  4. hen lamb is tender, remove from the oven and let rest, uncovered, while you preheat the broiler.
